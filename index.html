Term Decay and Mapping A suggestion is a sequence of terms with a specific ordering. Term decay determines how important this ordering is to result weighting. A high decay will emphasise proximal terms over distal terms. A low decay will place less empahsis on term ordering.
Term mappings describe how suggestion and search text is transformed prior to processing. Choice of mapping affects search characteristics and memory usage.

Term Edit Distance Hamming distance used when searching for individual terms. *Increasing distance may cause growth in processing time.
